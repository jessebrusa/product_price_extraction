Description

Upgrade your nighttime shooting setup with the Collector 320 Thermal Weapon Sight. It utilizes a durable, yet lightweight ULTEM Composite Body, giving it a weight of just under 1 pound to ensure a minimal effect on weapon handling characteristics. The heart of the Collector 320 is the exclusive ArmaCORE 320x240 12um Thermal Core – a powerful sensor that can detect a human sized heat signature at 708 meters, recognize the target at 177 meters, and identify the target at 107 meters. Heat sources are projected onto a high definition XGA 1024x768 OLED display, and are viewable in multiple color palettes. This allows the Collector 320 to be adapted to meet individual preferences or to specific environmental conditions. Other features include the Iron Wolf thermal technology platform. An extensive suite of features includes video and image capture, USB video stream, USB-C interface for viewing your recordings and photos on external devices such as computers or televisions.

Features of the Collector 320 Thermal Weapon Sight:

  • Multiple color palettes
  • Intuitive 3-button controlsv

  • Image Detail Enhancement
  • 1/2MOA Boresight

  • USB Video Stream

  • Inclinometer
